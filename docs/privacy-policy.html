Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how the AI Finance Factory application ("the App") operates and handles information in connection with the Pinterest Developer Platform.

2. Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or process personal data of end users. It is a content automation tool designed to publish content to Pinterest on behalf of the account owner.

3. Pinterest Access Token

The App uses a Pinterest Access Token solely for the purpose of publishing content to the authorized Pinterest account. The token is:

  • stored locally and never published or shared;
  • not transmitted to any third party except Pinterest's official API endpoints;
  • used only to perform actions explicitly requested by the account owner.

4. Data Sharing

The App does not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer user data to third parties. No personal information is collected, stored, or disclosed.

5. Content Published to Pinterest

The App may automatically generate and publish pins, articles, and other content to Pinterest. This content is created based on the account owner's configuration and does not contain personal data of third parties.

6. Security

The App stores credentials (Pinterest Access Token) locally on the user's machine and transmits them only over secure HTTPS connections to Pinterest's API. No credentials are logged or exposed in plain text.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
